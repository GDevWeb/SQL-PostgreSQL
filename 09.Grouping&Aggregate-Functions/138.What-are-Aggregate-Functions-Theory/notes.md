### what means aggregate ?

Mathematical operation returning a single (aggregate result)

### what means function ?

Predefined operation, which can be executed on demand

example :

```sql
SELECT SUM(salary) FROM Employee;
```

which are the aggregate fucntions ?

some examples :

- COUNT()
- SUM()
- MAX()
- MIN()
- AVERAGE()
